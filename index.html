Side-by-Side (SBS) Observations

Insights from 4 shadowing sessions conducted across 3 channels in February.

1. Strong Core Investigation

The team demonstrates a solid foundational ability to investigate issues, utilizing resources effectively to track down correct answers. Great proactive approach in organizing findings directly into notes to guide the workflow.

🎯 My Takeaway: The team knows how to look for information, giving us a solid, confident baseline to work from.

2. High Tech & Ops Proficiency

Good speed and confidence in operational/technical workflows. The team remains calm on calls, with excellent fast typing skills and strong grammar. It was great to see significant improvement from our newest members.

🎯 My Takeaway: Since basic mechanics are well-handled, coaching efforts can now shift toward more advanced skills.

3. Soft Skills & Assertiveness

Need support with customer-facing confidence and tone management. In some instances, the tone is passive (lacking a "smile" on the call), and there is noticeable discomfort when having to disagree with or educate a customer.

🎯 My Takeaway: Primary focus for upcoming training: communication nuances (professional pushback, confident education, warm tone).

March Focus: The Probation Sprint

With 6 agents completing probation this month, our absolute focus is balancing our newfound speed with accuracy and quality.
